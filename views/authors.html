About

Infologic *v1.01* was created to serve as a haven for anything information and information anything :). Well, that's if you understand humor. The site has a lot of potential and the frequent updates will bring out the beauty in it. So say this version is just the tip of the iceberg.

What to Expect

Well, expect a lot even from this version. Theres a chat section hidden in the discussion tab. Worldwide communication goes on in there. The developer took the idea and communication style of his favourite games: "codm, pubg". The blogs are fully updated and ready to be devoured by you.

Should I join?

Yes! Please do so. Plus if you have any passion for blogging, join as a writer. Welcome and help develop Infologic.
